This is an interesting case: while trying to locate the previous heart sticker on google street view, I could not find it, but I noticed another heart sticker on a traffic light post across the street lane. However, if that heart was there when I visited, I would have seen it.

So, after a bit of research it seems that the heart had been placed on the traffic light post back in 2015, and then in 2016 the post was probably hit by a car and became slightly slanted, so today there is no heart sticker on it anymore, but it is standing straight again, so it has probably been replaced.
